What is Survival Race?

Survival Race is an online multiplayer racing game that puts you in a fierce race in a hexagonal arena to test your skills against players around the world. The game features vivid 3D graphics with an arena made of small white hexagons that constantly change. Unlike many other racing titles, this one focuses on outlasting opponents in fast-paced gameplay and shrinking areas. The game has many features similar to IO games. Show off your skills and reflexes to win in this fierce arena!

How to Play Survival Race

Gameplay Overview

Survival Race Online takes place in an arena made up of hexagonal tiles that instantly crumble upon contact. Your mission is to control a car, navigate the platforms, collect coins, perform top-notch driving skills, and ram the rivals out of the arena without falling off the platforms. Your ultimate goal is to be the last survivor, collect as many coins as possible, and climb to the top of the leaderboard.

Detailed steps to survive in Survival Race

Entering the Hexagonal Arena

The arena is filled with other players. Your race begins as soon as you click or tap the screen to play.

Players must keep moving to avoid falling into gaps. Please note that the arena is composed of multiple hexagonal tiles, many of which will collapse or disappear during the gameplay (when a vehicle moves over them or when the arena gets smaller and smaller). The last player remaining on solid ground wins the match.

Using the Jump Feature

The jump ability is crucial for survival in this game. Time your jumps to make simple jumps or jump and somersault through the air to clear lost platforms or outmaneuver opponents.

Competing Against Opponents

This is a part of the multiplayer game. There are many online players around the world also participating in the arena with you. You can control your car to block or push others toward the collapsing tiles. However, sometimes, playing defensively and staying away from crowded areas of the arena is also a smart strategy.

Collecting Coins

Coins are scattered across the arena. Try to collect as many as you can to unlock new vehicles. Balancing coin collecting and survival is the way to stay in the game long enough to have a chance to own new vehicles.

Game Controls

  • Left mouse click to tap to start.
  • Steering: Left/right arrow keys or A, D
  • Jump: Up arrow, spacebar, or W

Tips and Tricks to Success in Survival Race

Keep moving and focus on steering: You can’t stop moving in the car game because the hexagonal platforms are constantly changing as they collapse or build up. In addition, opponents can also rush at you, so stay focused and keep moving to avoid all dangers and obstacles.

Stay central: Just falling into a hole, falling off the edge of a platform, and falling into the water will also fail you, so always move into the middle of stable platforms to increase your survival chances.

Master timing and use jumps (and roll jumps) wisely: Master the art of jumping and rolling your car to be able to avoid holes and opponents’ attacks when necessary.

Practice and Practice: Race Survival: Arena King game’s fast-paced nature requires not just strategic driving but also quick reflexes, so keep playing to improve.

FAQs

Is Survival Race free to play?

Yes, Survival Race Online is 100% free to play online at https://survival-race.io/. You can access and enjoy the game directly from your browser without any downloads or installations.

How to get a new car?

You can unlock new cars by collecting coins scattered throughout the arena, winning races, and kicking rivals. For example, you’ll get a new vehicle by having 10 coins. There are up to 160 new cars for you to unlock!

What happens if my car falls off the platform?

Falling off the arena eliminates you from the match, but you can jump into the next round to try again.

Does the game have offline modes?

No. Currently, the game focuses on online multiplayer action and requires an internet connection to play.
